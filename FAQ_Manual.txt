SECTION 1: ATM SERVICES FAQ
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q1.1: What should I do if the ATM doesn't dispense cash but debits my account?

A: This is a critical issue requiring immediate action:
1. Note the transaction reference number from the screen or receipt
2. Check your account balance within 2 hours via mobile banking
3. File a dispute immediately through our customer support (1-800-BANK-911)
4. Provisional credit will be issued within 24 hours
5. Investigation completes within 5-7 business days
6. Keep the ATM receipt as evidence

PRIORITY: High | RESOLUTION TIME: 24-48 hours | CATEGORY: ATM Services

Q1.2: My ATM card is stuck in the machine. How do I retrieve it?

A: Follow this immediate procedure:
1. Do NOT leave the ATM location if possible
2. Call our ATM helpline immediately: 1-800-ATM-HELP
3. Note the ATM location code (displayed on screen or machine)
4. Card will be retrieved by next cash replenishment (usually 24-48 hours)
5. Request temporary card issuance at nearest branch
6. File police report if ATM is in unsafe location

PRIORITY: Medium | RESOLUTION TIME: 24-48 hours | ESCALATION: ATM Operations Team

Q1.3: ATM screen is frozen during transaction. What now?

A: Technical freeze resolution:
1. Wait 60 seconds - system may auto-recover
2. Do NOT repeatedly press buttons
3. If frozen >2 minutes, contact support with ATM ID
4. Do NOT share PIN or card details over phone
5. Transaction will auto-reverse within 48 hours if incomplete
6. Check account after 4 hours for any unauthorized debits

PRIORITY: Medium | AUTO-REVERSAL: 48 hours | AFFECTED CUSTOMERS: Individual

Q1.4: ATM deposit not showing in my account. How long does it take?

A: Deposit processing timeline:
1. Cash deposits: Instant to 2 hours during banking hours
2. Check deposits: 1-2 business days for clearance
3. After-hours deposits: Next business day morning
4. Verify deposit receipt matches amount deposited
5. If delayed >4 hours, contact support with receipt number
6. Provisional credit available for regular customers

PRIORITY: Medium | STANDARD PROCESSING: 2-4 hours | SLA: Same business day

SECTION 2: INTERNET BANKING FAQ
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q2.1: Cannot login to internet banking. Password not working.

A: Login troubleshooting steps:
1. Verify Caps Lock is OFF
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Try different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge)
4. Use "Forgot Password" - reset link sent to registered email
5. Check if account is locked after 3 failed attempts (auto-unlock in 30 min)
6. If email not received, contact support for manual unlock

PRIORITY: Low-Medium | SELF-SERVICE: Yes | RESOLUTION: 5-15 minutes

Q2.2: Transaction history missing for the last few days. Where is it?

A: Transaction visibility issues:
1. Check if viewing correct account (current/savings)
2. Adjust date range filter - default shows last 30 days
3. Clear browser cache - may show cached old data
4. Batch processing runs every 4 hours - recent transactions may lag
5. Download statement for complete transaction list
6. If >24 hours missing, escalate to IT support

PRIORITY: Low | DATA SYNC: Every 4 hours | INVESTIGATION: 2-4 hours

Q2.3: Fund transfer failing with error code E303. What does it mean?

A: Error E303 indicates database connection timeout:
1. This is a temporary technical issue
2. Retry after 5 minutes
3. If persists, try during off-peak hours (10 PM - 6 AM)
4. Check system status page: status.bank.com
5. Contact support if urgent - manual transfer possible
6. Transaction will NOT be deducted if error occurs

PRIORITY: Medium | ERROR TYPE: Technical | RETRY: Automatic safe

Q2.4: Bill payment option not available for my biller. How to add?

A: Biller registration process:
1. Click "Add New Biller" in Bill Pay section
2. Search by biller name or category
3. If not listed, use "Add Custom Biller" option
4. Enter biller account number and confirm details
5. Verification takes 24-48 hours
6. Use NEFT/RTGS for immediate payment as alternative

PRIORITY: Low | PROCESSING TIME: 24-48 hours | WORKAROUND: Available

SECTION 3: MOBILE BANKING FAQ
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q3.1: Mobile app crashes immediately on startup. How to fix?

A: App crash troubleshooting:
1. Force close app and restart phone
2. Clear app cache: Settings > Apps > Bank App > Clear Cache
3. Check for app updates in Play Store/App Store
4. Uninstall and reinstall app (data won't be lost)
5. Ensure phone OS is updated to minimum required version
6. If persists, report bug with phone model to support

PRIORITY: Medium | DEVICE ISSUE: Possible | RESOLUTION: 15-30 minutes

Q3.2: Fingerprint authentication not working suddenly.

A: Biometric authentication fix:
1. Re-register fingerprint in phone settings
2. Clean fingerprint sensor on phone
3. Disable and re-enable biometric login in app settings
4. Use PIN/password as temporary login method
5. Check if phone OS was recently updated (may cause conflicts)
6. Some phone models not compatible - check compatibility list

PRIORITY: Low | WORKAROUND: PIN login | COMPATIBILITY: Check required

Q3.3: Push notifications for transactions not received.

A: Notification troubleshooting:
1. Check notification permissions: Settings > Apps > Bank App > Notifications
2. Ensure app is not in battery optimization/power saving mode
3. Check "Do Not Disturb" is not blocking app notifications
4. Toggle notifications OFF then ON in app settings
5. SMS alerts available as backup notification method
6. Email alerts can also be enabled in preferences

PRIORITY: Low | ALTERNATIVE: SMS/Email alerts | BATTERY OPTIMIZATION: Check

Q3.4: QR code scanner not opening in app.

A: Camera permission issue:
1. Grant camera permission: Settings > Apps > Bank App > Permissions
2. Check if another app is using camera
3. Restart phone and try again
4. Clear app cache and data
5. Use manual entry option for UPI/payment
6. Update app to latest version - bug may be fixed

PRIORITY: Low | WORKAROUND: Manual entry | PERMISSION: Required

SECTION 4: CREDIT CARD OPERATIONS FAQ
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q4.1: Card blocked due to suspected fraud. How to unblock?

A: Fraud block resolution:
1. Verify recent transactions via SMS/email alerts
2. Call fraud helpline: 1-800-FRAUD-00
3. Confirm legitimate transactions to lift block
4. If fraud confirmed, card will be replaced (3-5 days)
5. Report unauthorized transactions for chargeback
6. Temporary limit increase available while replacement ships

PRIORITY: High | RESPONSE TIME: Immediate | CARD REPLACEMENT: 3-5 days

Q4.2: Credit limit increase approved but not showing.

A: Limit update processing:
1. Limit updates process in batch (every 4 hours)
2. Check after next batch cycle
3. If urgent, request manual sync from customer support
4. Clear app/browser cache to see updated limit
5. Limit reflects on authorization system within 30 minutes
6. Physical card shows old limit but system uses new limit

PRIORITY: Low | BATCH PROCESSING: Every 4 hours | MANUAL SYNC: 15 minutes

Q4.3: Unauthorized transaction on my card. What to do?

A: Fraud transaction procedure:
1. Immediately block card via app/website or call support
2. File fraud dispute within 60 days of transaction
3. Provide transaction details and reason for dispute
4. Provisional credit issued within 10 business days
5. Investigation takes 30-45 days
6. Change all online shopping passwords
7. Consider fraud protection service

PRIORITY: Critical | BLOCK TIME: Immediate | PROVISIONAL CREDIT: 10 days

Q4.4: EMI conversion request pending for 2 weeks.

A: EMI conversion processing:
1. Standard processing time: 5-7 business days
2. Check if transaction is eligible (usually >$500 and <60 days old)
3. Ensure minimum 3 months tenure available on card validity
4. Contact support with transaction reference for status
5. If delayed, manual processing can be expedited
6. EMI appears on next billing cycle

PRIORITY: Low | STANDARD TIME: 5-7 days | ELIGIBILITY: Check required

SECTION 5: PAYMENT PROCESSING FAQ
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q5.1: ACH payment file upload validation failed. Common errors?

A: File validation checklist:
1. Verify NACHA standard format compliance
2. Check routing numbers are exactly 9 digits
3. Ensure account numbers don't exceed 17 characters
4. Remove special characters from beneficiary names
5. Verify file encoding is UTF-8 or ASCII
6. Check effective date is not in the past
7. Total debit/credit amounts must balance

PRIORITY: Medium | FILE FORMAT: NACHA | VALIDATION: Immediate

Q5.2: Direct debit mandate not reflecting in system.

A: Mandate activation process:
1. Mandate activation takes 24-48 hours after approval
2. Check if customer signed mandate form
3. Verify bank account is active and valid
4. Ensure mandate amount doesn't exceed account balance
5. Check effective date and expiry date
6. Force sync available for urgent cases

PRIORITY: Medium | ACTIVATION TIME: 24-48 hours | FORCE SYNC: Available

Q5.3: RTGS transaction stuck in processing for hours.

A: RTGS processing timeline:
1. RTGS operates in batches: 9 AM - 4:30 PM weekdays
2. Cut-off time: 4:30 PM for same-day processing
3. After hours transactions process next business day
4. Check beneficiary bank for receiving delays
5. Transaction tracking available via reference number
6. If stuck >2 hours during banking hours, escalate

PRIORITY: High | BATCH TIMING: Hourly | CUT-OFF: 4:30 PM

Q5.4: Payment gateway timeout during checkout. Money deducted?

A: Gateway timeout procedure:
1. Check bank SMS/email for debit confirmation
2. If debited, amount auto-refunds in 5-7 business days
3. Do NOT retry payment immediately (may duplicate)
4. Wait 15 minutes before retry
5. Contact merchant to confirm order status
6. File dispute if no refund after 7 days

PRIORITY: Medium | AUTO-REFUND: 5-7 days | DUPLICATE RISK: High

SECTION 6: LOAN MANAGEMENT FAQ
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q6.1: EMI auto-debit failed this month. What happens?

A: Failed EMI procedure:
1. Check account balance on EMI debit date
2. Verify auto-debit mandate is active
3. Pay manually immediately to avoid late fees
4. Late fee: $25-50 depending on loan amount
5. Credit score impact after 30 days overdue
6. Set up balance alerts before EMI date

PRIORITY: High | LATE FEE: $25-50 | GRACE PERIOD: 7 days

Q6.2: Loan closure request submitted 10 days ago. No update.

A: Loan closure timeline:
1. Standard processing: 5-7 business days
2. Verify all outstanding dues paid including charges
3. Check for any pending direct debits linked to loan
4. NOC (No Objection Certificate) generated after closure
5. Collateral release: Additional 7-10 days
6. Contact loan operations for status update

PRIORITY: Medium | PROCESSING: 5-7 days | NOC GENERATION: Automatic

Q6.3: Prepayment penalty calculation seems wrong.

A: Prepayment penalty verification:
1. Check loan agreement for prepayment terms
2. Usually 2-4% of outstanding principal
3. Some loans have no penalty after 6-12 months
4. RBI guidelines: No penalty for floating rate home loans
5. Request detailed calculation from loan team
6. Escalate if calculation doesn't match agreement

PRIORITY: Medium | VERIFY: Loan agreement | ESCALATION: Available

Q6.4: Interest rate change not applied to my loan.

A: Interest rate update process:
1. Rate changes apply from next billing cycle
2. Floating rates update automatically per RBI changes
3. Fixed rate loans: No change during tenure
4. Check amortization schedule for updated rate
5. Rate change notification sent via email/SMS
6. Request fresh loan statement showing new rate

PRIORITY: Low | UPDATE CYCLE: Monthly | VERIFICATION: Statement

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q7.1: Core banking system showing "Service Unavailable" error.

A: System downtime handling:
1. Check system status: status.bank.com
2. Planned maintenance windows: Sundays 1-5 AM
3. Emergency maintenance: Announced via SMS/email
4. Use mobile app as alternative during downtime
5. Critical transactions: Visit branch or call support
6. SLA: 99.5% uptime excluding maintenance windows

PRIORITY: Critical if widespread | MAINTENANCE: Sunday 1-5 AM | SLA: 99.5%

Q7.2: Account balance showing incorrectly.

A: Balance discrepancy resolution:
1. Verify available balance vs. ledger balance
2. Check for pending transactions/holds
3. Clear browser cache and re-login
4. Download account statement for verification
5. Batch processing may delay balance updates (max 4 hours)
6. If discrepancy >$100 or >24 hours, escalate immediately

PRIORITY: High | VERIFICATION: Statement | ESCALATION: >$100 or >24hrs

Q7.3: End of day processing taking longer than usual.

A: EOD processing monitoring:
1. Standard EOD time: 11 PM - 2 AM
2. Large transaction volumes may extend to 4 AM
3. Critical services remain available during EOD
4. Batch reports available by 6 AM
5. If EOD fails, rollback and retry scheduled
6. Escalate to core banking team if delayed >6 AM

PRIORITY: Critical for operations | STANDARD TIME: 11 PM - 2 AM | MONITORING: 24/7

SECTION 8: FRAUD & SECURITY FAQ
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q8.1: Received suspicious email asking for account details.

A: Phishing email identification:
1. NEVER click links in suspicious emails
2. Bank NEVER asks for PIN/password via email
3. Check sender email domain carefully (look for misspellings)
4. Forward to phishing@bank.com for investigation
5. Delete email and mark as spam
6. Change password if you clicked any links
7. Enable 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication)

PRIORITY: High (Security) | ACTION: Do NOT click | REPORT: phishing@bank.com

Q8.2: False fraud alert blocking legitimate purchase.

A: Fraud alert override:
1. Confirm you're making the transaction
2. Call fraud team to verify and whitelist merchant
3. Provide transaction details and merchant name
4. One-time approval granted immediately
5. Add merchant to trusted list for future
6. Consider pre-travel notification for foreign transactions

PRIORITY: Medium | WHITELIST: Immediate | PRE-TRAVEL: Recommended

Q8.3: Suspicious login attempt detected. Account at risk?

A: Login security procedure:
1. Immediately change password if not you
2. Review recent login history in security settings
3. Enable 2FA if not already active
4. Check for unauthorized devices in session management
5. Revoke access to unknown devices
6. Consider fraud monitoring service enrollment

PRIORITY: High | PASSWORD CHANGE: Immediate | 2FA: Strongly recommended

Q8.4: Card cloning suspected. What immediate steps?

A: Card cloning response:
1. Block card immediately via app or call 1-800-BLOCK-IT
2. Review last 30 days transactions for unauthorized activity
3. File police report with FIR number
4. Request card replacement with new number
5. Dispute fraudulent transactions within 60 days
6. Consider EMV chip card upgrade
7. Never share CVV or OTP with anyone

PRIORITY: Critical | BLOCK: Immediate | REPLACEMENT: 3-5 days | FIR: Required

Q9.1: Regulatory report submission deadline approaching. Format issues?

A: Regulatory submission checklist:

3. Validate against XSD schema before submission
4. Check all mandatory fields populated
5. Test submission with sample file first
6. Submit 24 hours before deadline for contingency
7. Keep submission confirmation for audit trail

Q9.2: AML (Anti-Money Laundering) report generation errors.

A: AML reporting troubleshooting:
1. Verify customer KYC data is complete
2. Check transaction threshold settings
3. Ensure date range covers reporting period
4. Validate suspicious transaction flagging logic
5. Include all account types per regulations
6. Generate test report for validation
7. Escalate to compliance team if errors persist

PRIORITY: High | COMPLIANCE: Mandatory | THRESHOLD: Per RBI norms

Q9.3: KYC compliance report showing data gaps.

A: KYC data completion:
1. Identify missing fields in customer profiles
2. Priority: PAN, Aadhaar, Address proof
3. Initiate customer outreach for document submission
4. Use video KYC for remote verification
5. Set deadline for submission (usually 30 days)
6. Freeze accounts if non-compliant per regulations
7. Track completion rate for compliance reporting

PRIORITY: High | MANDATORY DOCS: PAN, Aadhaar | ACCOUNT FREEZE: If non-compliant

Q9.4: CTR (Cash Transaction Report) data validation failed.

Q10.1: How to escalate an unresolved support ticket?

A: Escalation path:
Level 1 (L1): Initial support team - Response: 2-4 hours
Level 2 (L2): Technical specialists - Response: 4-8 hours
Level 3 (L3): System engineers - Response: 8-24 hours
Level 4 (L4): Vendor support - Response: 24-48 hours
Management: Critical issues only - Response: Immediate

PRIORITY: Based on issue | SLA: Per level | TRACKING: Ticket number

Q10.2: Support ticket not resolved within SLA. What now?

A: SLA breach procedure:
1. Check ticket status and last update time
2. Contact assigned support agent directly
3. Request escalation to next level
4. File complaint if SLA breach >24 hours
5. Compensation policy may apply per agreement
6. Critical issues: Call emergency hotline

PRIORITY: Per ticket | SLA TRACKING: Automated | COMPENSATION: Policy-based

Q10.3: Need historical ticket data for audit purposes.

A: Ticket history retrieval:
1. Access support portal with admin credentials
2. Export tickets by date range, category, status
3. Available formats: CSV, Excel, PDF
4. Retention period: 7 years per regulations
5. Include attachments if needed
6. Request via support@bank.com for bulk export

PRIORITY: Low | RETENTION: 7 years | EXPORT: Self-service available

Q10.4: How to provide feedback on support quality?

A: Feedback mechanisms:
1. CSAT survey sent after ticket closure
2. Rate agent performance (1-5 stars)
3. Comment on resolution quality and timeliness
4. Feedback reviewed by QA team weekly
5. Positive feedback: Agent recognition
6. Negative feedback: Retraining and process improvement

PRIORITY: Low | SURVEY: Automatic | REVIEW: Weekly
